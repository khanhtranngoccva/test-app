A sleek, minimalistic and thematic Pomodoro to calm your mind and boost your day!

What is Tranquil?

Tranquil is a minimalistic, thematic and beautiful Pomodoro timer that works on desktops and mobile browser. This app will help you focus on any tasks with the help of minimalistic themes and hand-picked white noises designed to help you focus your best.

What is the Pomodoro technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is created by Francesco Cirillo for a more productive way to work and study. The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. Each interval is known as a pomodoro, from the Italian word for 'tomato', after the tomato-shaped kitchen timer that Cirillo used as a university student. - Wikipedia

Tranquil Features

  • Beautiful themes, transitions and sounds, inspired by @saviomartin7. More are coming! Feel free to drop suggestions for more themes on Twitter!

  • Completely customizable timer, breaks and long breaks!

  • Built-in to-do list that allows you to keep track of your work.

  • Want to stop phubbing? Click away and we'll remind you to come back! (You need to turn on browser notifications!)

  • We'll tell you when to take a break. (You'll need to turn on browser notifications for that too!)

How to use Tranquil

  • Add project tasks to work on in the homepage. Estimate how many Pomodoros you need with the bottom bar!

  • Pick a task to work on and press Start to begin the Pomodoro!

  • Focus on the task for 25 minutes. No distractions!

  • Take a 5-minute break.

  • Every 4 breaks, you get a 15-minute long break.

  • Iterate until you finish the task! Then mark it as complete.
